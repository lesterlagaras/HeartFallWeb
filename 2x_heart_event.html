🎉 HeartFall SMP – 2X Heart Event 🎉

Attention HeartFall Adventurers!

We’re excited to announce our 2X Heart Event — a limited-time event where all player kills will grant double hearts! 💖

🕒 Event Details

  • Duration: 15 minutes per event
  • Automatic: Starts every 30 minutes
  • Manual Start: Server OPs can start anytime using /2xheart start
  • Countdown: Actionbar countdown shows the remaining time
  • Bonus: Each kill gives +1 extra heart during the event

💡 How It Works

  • Automatic Event: Every 30 minutes, the event starts automatically. Players will see an actionbar countdown.
  • Manual Event: OPs can trigger the event anytime using /2xheart start.
  • During the Event: Killing other players grants double hearts. Actionbar shows remaining time.
  • Joining Late: Players who join during an active event will see the actionbar countdown immediately.

⚡ Tips

  • Prepare before the event starts to maximize your bonus hearts!
  • Take advantage of kills during the 15-minute window to get ahead in survival and PvP.
  • Keep an eye on the actionbar countdown — it’s your timer for the event!
  • Get ready, gear up, and may the strongest survive! 💥
